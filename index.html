Progressive Enhancement

What I got from reading about Progressive Enhancement, was that it was a way to start very simple and then progressively add complexity to it. It enforces the idea to start at focusing on content, then to making it presentable, and finally the client-side scripting. The graceful degradation perspective focuses on building the website for the most advanced browsers out there on the internet. In doing this youre not really focused on the older broswers.
